The "recent_bcpr_gse_cohorts.csv" contains Current Balances and BCPR3 for two different mortgage products and GSEs for the first two months of 2024.  The following is the data dictionary for the data set.


"GSE"=A factor variable with 2 classes, defining the corresponding Government Sponsored Enterprise
"Product"=A factor variable with 2 classes, defining the corresponding duration of the Fixed Rate Mortgage
"Activity Month"=The month in which the prepayment activity occured
"Current Balance"=The outstanding unpaid principal balance at the end of the Activity Month
"BCPR3"=The 3-Month Benchmarked Conditional Prepayment Rate
